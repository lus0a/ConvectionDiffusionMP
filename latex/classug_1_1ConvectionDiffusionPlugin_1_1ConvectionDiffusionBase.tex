\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename TDomain$>$\newline
class ug\+::\+Convection\+Diffusion\+Plugin\+::\+Convection\+Diffusion\+Base$<$ TDomain $>$}
Discretization for the Convection-\/\+Diffusion Equation. 

This class implements the IElem\+Disc interface to provide element local assemblings for the convection diffusion equation. The Equation has the form  \[
    \partial_t (m_1 c + m_2) - \nabla \cdot \left ( D \nabla c - \vec{v} c - \vec{F} \right )
        + r_1 \cdot c + r_2 = f + \nabla \cdot \vec{f}_2
\] with 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $ c $ is the unknown solution 
\item $ m_1 \equiv m_1(\vec{x},t) $ is the Mass Scaling Term 
\item $ m_2 \equiv m_1(\vec{x},t) $ is the Mass Term 
\item $ D \equiv D(\vec{x},t) $ is the Diffusion Tensor 
\item $ v \equiv \vec{v}(\vec{x},t) $ is the Velocity Field 
\item $ F \equiv \vec{F}(\vec{x},t) $ is the Flux 
\item $ r_1 \equiv r_1(\vec{x},t) $ is the Reaction Rate 
\item $ r_2 \equiv r_2(\vec{x},t) $ is a Reaction Term 
\item $ f \equiv f(\vec{x},t) $ is a Source Term 
\item $ \vec{f}_2 \equiv \vec{f}_2(\vec{x},t) $ is a Vector Source Term 
\end{DoxyItemize}
